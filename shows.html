Shows and more

Open Stage / Watch for free

We would love to a create space for dancers not only to learn, dance, listen, create and discuss, but also to show their own dance! You can apply your dance video to the Open Stage, there are only a few conditions:

  • The dance has to be in the style of Golden Era bellydance, Golden Era inspired, or vintage oriental fantasy.
  • The video must not be older than 1 year
  • The maximum length must be 3,5 mins
  • The participant has to buy minimum 2 festival workshops
  • The video has to be uploaded until 26 February 2021 to a specific link (that will be sent to you after your application)
There are only 25 spots, don’t hesitate and apply now. The show will have its premiere on Saturday 13 March 00:30 CET on Kan Zaman’s YouTube channel.

Golden Era and Modern Times - Discussion for free

Comparing Golden Era of bellydance with modern bellydance, one can come up with thousands of questions. Most of them do not have a simple answer but are worth discussing. Is it necessary to learn about history of Raqs Sharqi? Is studying Golden Era dance important if you are interested in modern and fusion bellydance? Is modern bellydance less artistic and elegant than in Golden Era? What is the difference in appearance of bellydance in media now and then? Shahrzad, Julia Farid, Shruti, and Nisaa will discuss these and other intriguing questions.

Sunday 14th March at 21:00 CET
