Welcome to a fairy tale, at Kan Zaman,
Golden Era Music & Dance Festival

Kan Zaman means ‘Once upon a time’, something you would say at the beginning of a fairy tale. And we sincerely hope this festival will be as beautiful as a fairy tale for you.

We have carefully prepared a program full of lectures, dance workshops, tutorials, concerts, and we will have even a museum tour, watch party, discussion, and two shows. Being online gives us a great possibility to offer you recordings of the workshops, in case you cannot attend the workshop live. Therefore, everyone who is sign in for a workshop will receive a link to attend the workshop live, and the recording within several days after the live workshop. The recording will stay up for 2 weeks, so you can take your time in watching it.
